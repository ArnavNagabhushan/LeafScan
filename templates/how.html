How LeafScan Works

Step-by-step process to detect plant leaf diseases using AI

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Upload a Leaf Image: Take a clear photo of your plant leaf and upload it.
  2. Image Processing: LeafScan resizes and preprocesses the image for AI analysis.
  3. Model Prediction: Our trained Keras model compares your leaf with thousands of images of healthy and diseased leaves.
  4. Detection & Confidence: The AI predicts the plant type, detects disease (if any), and gives a confidence percentage.
