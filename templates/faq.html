General Questions

  • Q: What is LeafScan?
    A: LeafScan is an AI-based web application that detects plant leaf diseases from uploaded images.
  • Q: What kinds of plants can LeafScan detect diseases for?
    A: Our model is trained for multiple plants such as tomato, potato, bell pepper, peach, orange, strawberry, and more. We support 22 different plant-disease combinations!
  • Q: What file formats are supported?
    A: You can upload PNG, JPG, or JPEG image files.
  • Q: How accurate is the prediction?
    A: The accuracy depends on the quality of the image and the dataset used to train the model. Our goal is over 95% accuracy.
  • Q: Why is my image not uploading?
    A: Please ensure you are uploading a valid image file and that its size is not too large.
  • Q: Can LeafScan be used offline?
    A: Currently, it is an online web app that requires an internet connection to process images.
  • Q: Will more crops be supported in the future?
    A: Yes! The next version of LeafScan will support more plant species and even mobile app integration.
